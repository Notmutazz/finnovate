AI-Driven Microfinance for Students

Finnovate is an AI-driven microfinance platform designed for students at Presidency University, offering instant, secure, and fair peer-to-peer loans. Using AI to assess eligibility and blockchain for transparency, it enables seamless borrowing, lending, and investing through an in-app wallet. Users earn FinnoPoints for transactions, redeemable on campus, while Fiona AI provides smart financial insights and investment guidance. With fast processing, no direct bank links, and student-focused rewards, Finnovate empowers financial independence and smart money management.
